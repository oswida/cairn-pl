# Basilisk
6 HP, 12 STR, 13 DEX, bite (d10)  
- Long, magical, serpentine lizards that dwell in caverns.  
- Anyone who looks directly at them starts to get petrified.  
- Critical damage: the victim gets petrified (d8+d8 DEX damage).
