# Wolf, Normal
3 HP, 14 DEX, bite (d6)
- Dwell primarily in wildlands, but occasionally lair in caves.
-   Can be trained like dogs if captured young.
-   When found in packs of at least 4, treat WIL as 15 for morale
    checks.
