# Elemental, Air
16 HP, 11 STR, 15 DEX, 8 WIL, wind blow (d8)  
- Huge, living vortexes of whirling air.  
- Sweep away lighter creatures with their strong winds.  
- Harm aerial creatures by disrupting their flight.
