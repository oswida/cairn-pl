# Cat, Lion
5 HP, 12 STR, 12 DEX, bite (d10)  
- Hunts in groups called pride. Live in savannahs or other similar drylands.  
- Carnivorous. Prefer to eat bovines but might hunt even humans.  
- Always chase prey that flees.
