# Blood Elk
4 HP, 12 STR, 13 DEX, 5 WIL, horns (d8)
- Born from a greedy, selfish act.
- Attacks & kills to eat meat, but gains no sustenance.
- Critical damage: gores its victims by ripping out their entrails.
