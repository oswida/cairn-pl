# Snake, Pit Viper
3 HP, 12 DEX, bite (d4)
-   5' long snakes with greyish scales, found in caves and other dark
    places.
-   Does not rely on vision, instead, senses its victims by their body
    heat.
-   Critical Damage: The poison kills the target in 2d4 turns if not
    treated.
