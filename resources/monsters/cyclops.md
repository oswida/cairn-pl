# Cyclops
13 HP, 18 STR, 8 DEX, 6 WIL, club (d10)  
- Giant humanoids with a single large eye centered on the face.   Dwell in caves and herd small animals.  
- Slow-witted, can easily be fooled by other intelligent beings.
